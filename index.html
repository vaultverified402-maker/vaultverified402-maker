How Verification Works
A record exists the moment it's filed. Not after it's proven right.
01
Filed before the event
Every selection is written to the public record before first pitch, tipoff, or kickoff with a timestamp that cannot be altered.
02
Locked on submission
Once preserved, a record cannot be edited or removed. Corrections are filed as new entries, never rewrites.
03
Graded against the result
Outcomes are recorded from official box scores after the event closes, win or lose, and attached permanently to the original entry.
04
Public and permanent
The full history stays visible. Every record. Every outcome. Nothing is curated after the fact.
